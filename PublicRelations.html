Public Relations


Public Relations plays a key role in spreading the team’s ideals to new communities and informing other students about the world of robotics and what the team does. Public relations members attend and organize events to spread the word about robotics. They focus on getting the team as many sponsors mentors, and supporters as possible, in order to keep the team alive and strong. The department is also responsible for writing essays and producing a website which is required for all teams to have each year. The essays are for various awards which can be won by teams, some more prestigious than others. All in all, this department is as important as any other department.
